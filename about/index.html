Welcome to my homepage! I’m Hory, an open source enthusiast, yet, a Java developer. This should not be the first time I have set up a private website. As a person who is not used to improvising, I hope that my homepage can also keep up with the times, not just my technology, so this will not be the last time.

The content of this blog is mainly based on various topics. I choosed a relatively concise topic when I set up the blog, in order to bring readers a better reading experience. The advantage of the series of articles is that it can better establish the reader’s grasp of a knowledge system, which is not available in other blog sites. Of course, if you think these articles are practical or even want to learn about some of my other technical articles, you can click the buttons in the navigation bar such as Github/Gitee to jump to the corresponding site, where there is more abundant content.
